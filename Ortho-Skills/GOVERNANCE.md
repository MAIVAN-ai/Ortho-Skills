# Governance

## Mission

Maintain an open, surgeon-supervised, safety-aware orthopaedic AI skill library that improves case documentation, classification consistency, clinical evidence workflows, education, and outcomes tracking.

## Maintainer roles

- Clinical maintainer
- Technical maintainer
- Evidence maintainer
- Safety maintainer
- Data-governance maintainer

## External-source policy

This repository may refer to external standards and websites. It must not reproduce copyrighted materials beyond what is legally permitted. Contributors should link to official sources rather than copying figures, diagrams, or substantial text.
